What we do at EcoParali


ENCOURAGING SUSTAINABLE WELL-BEING & GOVERNANCE


Safeguard the Ecosystem

We safeguard the ecosystem by reducing air pollution from stubble burning, improving soil health, and promoting biodiversity. Through eco-friendly disposal methods, we ensure sustainable agricultural practices, mitigating environmental risks and fostering a healthier environment.

Promote Sustainability

By transforming stubble into valuable resources for eco-friendly products, we connect farmers with industries, and reduce reliance on non-renewable materials. We support a circular economy and drive sustainable agricultural practices, benefiting both the environment and economy.

Make Many Lives Better
We improve farmers' lives by providing additional income through the sale of stubble, simplify stubble management, reducing labor and time, and help farmers avoid penalties associated with stubble burning. We empowers farmers with sustainable practices, enhancing their financial stability and quality of life.

Who we are

Get to know about Paryavaran Pioneers

We are four engineering students who transformed a real-life problem into actionable solutions. We conducted ground-level surveys and engaged directly with farmers and gram panchayats to understand the challenges of stubble management. Our hands-on approach led to the development of EcoParali, a platform for sustainable alternatives to stubble burning, improving farmers' livelihoods, and promoting a greener rural India.
